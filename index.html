Who is she?

As stated at the top, Anika is a rising sophomore at Denmark High School. She has diverse experiences from her first year of high school. She played on two varsity teams, the swim team and tennis team. From this, she gained skills on how to work with others and how to contribute to a team. In addition, she has learned how to overcome challenges and deal with emotion.

Adding on to her skills, Anika particpated in her schools theatre department. She did the spring show playing Mabel in the show Fame Jr. Through this experience, she gained public speaking skills that can help in the workplace.

Anika is a member of her Girls Who Code and Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA) chapter at her school. At the state leadership conference, she and her team competed in Website Design and placed first. Doing this has given her the skills to make a website for a business.
